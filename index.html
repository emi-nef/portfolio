About Me

I'm Nicola Emilia Forghani, but I go by Emi. I was born in Chicago and raised in Southern California, USA. It's hard to summarize a person, but I can tell you I enjoy spending time outside, usually practicing archery, hiking, or fishing, I like listening to music, e.g. CAKE and Pink Floyd, I love learning about all sorts of things, but especially about how humans functioned in the world before modern technology. Some other things I enjoy include sewing, writing, video games, and organizing.

I've been exposed to coding my whole life by virtue of growing up with an internet connection. As a kid, I often dug through hidden files, messed around in DevTools, and tried to use the terminal, even going so far as to install Ubuntu on my laptop, when I got one. However, I truly began my journey into coding when I joined UC Berkeley's coding bootcamp through edX in the summer of 2023.
